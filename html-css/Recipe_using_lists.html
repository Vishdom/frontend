Egg Fried Recipe

Here is a recipe to make delicious egg fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice- 2 cups (preferably a day old or cold rice)
  • Eggs- 2
  • Green Chillis- 2
  • Cooking Oil- 4 to 5 table spoons
  • Salt- to taste
  • Garam Masala- half a tea spoon
  • Vegetables(optional)- 1 cup(bell peppers, carrot, beans, corn, or a mix chopped small)

Instructions

  1. Prepare your ingredients
    • chop your vegetables into small uniform pieces so that they cook evenly.
    • Beat the eggs in a bowl with a pinch of salt
  2. Heat the pan/wok
    • Place a wok or large frying pan over medium-high heat.
    • Add 4 tablespoons of cooking oil and let it heat until shimmering.
  3. Cook the vegetables
    • Add the chopped vegetables to the hot oil along with chopped green chillis.
    • Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until they are slightly tender but still crisp.
  4. Scramble the eggs
    • Push the vegetables to one side of the pan.
    • Pour the beaten eggs into the empty side.
    • Let the eggs cook for a few seconds, then gently scramble until they are just set.
  5. Add Cooked Rice
    • Break up any clumps of cooked rice and add it to the pan.
    • Mix the rice with the vegetables and eggs thoroughly.
  6. Season the rice
    • Sprinkle salt to taste.
    • Add ½ teaspoon of garam masala (adjust if you want it more flavorful).
    • Stir-fry everything together for 2–3 minutes, ensuring the rice is heated through.
